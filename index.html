Mission Statement

Raby's Sadaqa is a humanitarian association committed to bringing hope, comfort, and vital support to sick and disadvantaged children, with a primary focus on Senegal, but extending its reach to the United States, Canada, and beyond. We provide essential resources—clothing, shoes, food, medication, toiletries, and more—while also offering emotional and moral support through hospital visits and sponsorship programs for orphans. Our mission is rooted in compassion, aiming to empower children and foster sustainable well-being within vulnerable communities.
